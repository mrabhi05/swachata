About Us

An initiative to make a platform to bring citizens , NGOs and the Government together to work for the common cause namely, a healthy and clean society.

Uncleanliness has become a social issue nowadays. It has become the root cause of various illnesses. It also destroys the aesthetic beauty of our natural surroundings. Hence, a clean society is the need of the hour. So as IT Engineers, Swachhata movement is our way of contributing to the society's well-being by utilizing our potential as the youth of today to create a cleaner, healthier and beautiful environment for a brighter tomorrow. This initiative also complements the Swachh Bharat Mission undertaken by the Indian Government. Our Goals are the 3Cs namely:

Community Events

The community as a whole can also organize and participate in various events and drives by viewing their details in one place. Our aim is to motivate people to come forward with concrete ideas to keep our city clean thus changing our surroundings for the better through collective efforts of the common man and different organizations by inculcating responsibility towards our environment in everyone.

Complaint Registration

Facilitate the process of registering complaints pertaining to unclean surroundings especially those requiring immediate attention.

Common Welfare

Gather like-minded people together on a web platform to work for a common cause.
